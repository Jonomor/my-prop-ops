## Legal Product Definition

As stated in our Terms of Service (Section 3):

> **MyPropOps is provided as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscription. Users are granted a LICENSE to use the software, NOT ownership of the software itself.**

The license granted is:
- **Limited** - Restricted to specified use cases
- **Non-exclusive** - May be granted to other users
- **Non-transferable** - Cannot be assigned or sublicensed
- **Revocable** - May be terminated for breach
- **Subscription-based** - Valid only during active subscription

## Business Registration Checklist

- [ ] Register business entity (LLC/Corp) in state of incorporation
- [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS
- [ ] Register NAICS code 513210 with Secretary of State
- [ ] File trademark applications (Class 42)
- [ ] Register copyright for software
- [ ] Obtain business insurance (Tech E&O, Cyber Liability)
- [ ] Register with state tax authorities for SaaS sales tax (where applicable)

## SaaS Sales Tax Nexus

Many states now require sales tax collection for SaaS products. States with SaaS taxation include:
- Texas, New York, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Connecticut, and others

**Recommendation:** Consult with a tax professional about sales tax obligations based on where your customers are located.
