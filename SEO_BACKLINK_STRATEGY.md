## Part 2: Anchor Text Strategy

### Analysis Summary
Yardi's most successful anchor text patterns fall into these categories:

### Category 1: Legal/Policy Pages (Lowest Priority for Acquisition)
These are auto-generated from product footers and don't require active pursuit.
- "Terms of Service" - 54,361 links
- "Privacy Statement" - 39,774 links
- "Privacy Policy" - 37,022 links
- "Accessibility Statement" - 18,956 links

**MyPropOps Action:** Ensure all legal pages exist and are linked from the footer of any integrations/partnerships.
