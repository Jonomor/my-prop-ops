AUTHORIZATION FOR BACKGROUND CHECK

Purpose: This form authorizes the property manager/landlord to conduct a background check as part of your housing application. This is a standard requirement for most housing programs including Section 8, HUD, and LIHTC.

Applicant Information

Authorization

I hereby authorize the property management company and/or landlord to obtain a consumer report and/or investigative consumer report on me. This report may include:

Criminal history records
Sex offender registry check
Eviction history
Previous rental history verification
Employment verification

I understand that:

  • This authorization is valid for the duration of my tenancy application
  • I have the right to request disclosure of the nature and scope of any investigation
  • I may request a copy of any report obtained
  • If any adverse action is taken based on the report, I will be notified

Certification

I certify that the information provided above is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge. I understand that any false statements may result in denial of my application.

This form complies with the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
