AUTHORIZATION FOR CREDIT CHECK

Purpose: This form authorizes the property manager/landlord to obtain your credit report as part of your housing application. Credit checks help verify financial responsibility and are standard practice for rental applications.

Applicant Information

Authorization Statement

I hereby authorize the property management company and/or landlord named below to obtain my credit report from one or more of the three major credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion) for the purpose of evaluating my rental application.

Your Rights Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)

  • You have the right to receive a copy of any credit report obtained
  • If your application is denied based on credit information, you will be notified
  • You have the right to dispute any inaccurate information in your credit report
  • You can obtain a free copy of your credit report annually at annualcreditreport.com

Acknowledgment

I understand this is a "soft pull" or "hard pull" credit inquiry (circle one)
I understand I may be charged an application fee for this service
I certify all information provided is accurate and complete
