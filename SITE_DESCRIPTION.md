## Full Description

### About MyPropOps

MyPropOps is the industry-leading cloud-based property management platform designed to transform how property managers, landlords, and housing operators run their businesses. Our Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution provides a comprehensive suite of tools that eliminate operational inefficiencies, ensure regulatory compliance, and scale with your portfolio.

### Why Property Managers Choose MyPropOps

**Streamlined Operations**
Replace your scattered spreadsheets, missed deadlines, and compliance headaches with one intuitive dashboard. MyPropOps centralizes property management, tenant tracking, inspection workflows, and document storage in a single platform your entire team can access.

**Built for Scale**
Whether you manage 2 properties or 200, MyPropOps grows with you. Our subscription tiers are designed to match your portfolio size, from free accounts for small landlords to enterprise solutions for large property management companies.

**Industry-Leading Features**
- **Property & Unit Management** - Track unlimited properties, occupancy status, and unit details
- **Tenant Portal** - Give tenants a secure portal to submit requests and upload documents
- **Inspection Workflows** - Schedule, assign, and track inspections for 100% compliance
- **Maintenance Requests** - Manage work orders from submission to completion
- **Document Vault** - Securely store leases, IDs, and inspection reports
- **Team Collaboration** - Invite your team with role-based access controls
- **Smart Notifications** - Never miss a lease renewal or inspection deadline

**Trusted Security**
Your data is protected with industry-standard AES-256 encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and role-based access controls. We're committed to keeping your property and tenant information safe.
